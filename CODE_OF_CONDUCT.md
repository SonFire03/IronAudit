# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, professional, and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Focus discussions on technical substance
- Accept feedback professionally

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or inflammatory behavior
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject content that violates this code.
Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.
